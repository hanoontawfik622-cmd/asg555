Who is ASG ?

The Future Of Engineering Industries

Zamzam for Engineer Industries was established in 2002 in Egypt. we started with a complete german line for manufacturing round spiral ducts and fittings. Over the time our range of products has grown to include a very wide range of products such as HVAC ducts (round and rectangular) made of black steel, galvanized steel, stainless steel, aluminum, air outlets, dampers, silencers, terminal boxes, VAV, duct heaters. we also produce cable management system (cable trays, cable ladder and cable trunk).

Our Vision

To be the preferred partner for HVAC consultants and contractors in the Middle East by delivering quality products, on-time delivery and responsive after-sales service.

Our Mission

To design and manufacture efficient, reliable and safe air-distribution systems that improve indoor comfort and protect buildings.

Our Values

Quality, innovation, safety, customer satisfaction and environmental responsibility.
